The Teesside Parmo

Description

Parmo, or Teesside Parmesan, is a dish originating in Middlesbrough, North Yorkshire, and a popular item of take-away food in the Teesside area.

Ingredients

  • breaded cutlet of chicken or pork
  • topped with a white béchamel sauce and cheese, usually cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Take the piece of flattened pork or chicken fillet, beat the eggs
  2. Dip fillet into the egg and then the breadcrumbs to coat, then deep fry, brown fillet on both sides.
  3. Add béchamel sauce and parmesan cheese and place under the grill.
